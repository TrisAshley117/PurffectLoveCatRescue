Our veterinary partners play an important role in providing low cost spay/neuter and other medical care.  After the cats are trapped, they are transferred to Alabama Spay and Neuter where they are spayed, neutered, given rabies shots, and ear-tipped. Cats that need additional medical treatment are examined at one of three vet clinics. After a period of recovery, the cats are returned to their colonies.
