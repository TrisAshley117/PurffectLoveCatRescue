After they have been socialized, we house the adoptable cats and kittens at Pet Supermarket in Vestavia where they stay for about a three-to-four week period. If not adopted, they spend time with a foster parent and receive further socialization; then after about a month break, they are returned to the store where, we hope, they will be adopted into their permanent home.
