Purrfect Love Cat Rescue

Thank you for contacting us! If needed, you will hear back within 48-72 hours.

Managing and caring for feral cat colonies requires dedication as well as time.  Whether it's hot or cold, raining or snowing, the cats must be given food and water daily, and we are always looking for compassionate, willing volunteers to help us!  If you would be interested in volunteering once a week at any of our feral cat colonies in Jefferson and Shelby counties, please fill out the contact information below. We would welcome your help and so would our feral cats!
