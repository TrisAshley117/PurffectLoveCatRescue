There are approximately 60 million feral cats in the United States. Facing predators, disease, and starvation, those cats that survive in the wild can produce an estimated 40 million kittens each year and thus start this devastating cycle again. At Purrfect Love Cat Rescue, we are dedicated to the rescue and rehabilitation of these abandoned and abused cats and kittens through Trap-Neuter-Return (TNR) and adoptions . . .
